Welcome to AIMS Setup
=====================

AIMS (by iBit Ltd) helps your team manage attendance, tasks,
screen activity, chat, and reports in one modern workspace.

Before you continue, please note:

  • Windows 10 or later (64-bit) is required
  • Administrator rights are needed for this installer
  • An internet connection is recommended for first sign-in
  • Visual C++ Runtime and Windows Media components may be
    configured automatically if missing

You will be asked to accept the License Agreement on the next
screens. After installation you can launch AIMS from the
Start Menu or Desktop shortcut (if selected).
